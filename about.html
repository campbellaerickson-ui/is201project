About the Resorts

Which should you choose?

Snowbird is known for its steep terrain, deep powder, and long ski season, making it a top choice for advanced and adventurous skiers. Its iconic tram provides fast access to incredible vertical and some of Utahâ€™s best big-mountain terrain. With consistent storms and excellent snow quality, Snowbird offers challenging runs and unforgettable powder days. Choose Snowbird if you want high-adrenaline skiing and world-class terrain.

Alta is a legendary, skiers-only resort famous for having some of the lightest, driest powder on earth. Its laid-back, classic ski culture and stunning natural beauty create a timeless mountain experience. With diverse terrain and frequent snowstorms, Alta is perfect for strong intermediates and experts who love deep powder and a pure skiing environment. Choose Alta if you want authentic, powder-centric skiing without the frills.

Deer Valley is a luxury, guest-focused resort offering impeccably groomed runs, exceptional service, and a refined mountain atmosphere. Known for limited lift tickets, it keeps crowds low and provides one of the smoothest, most comfortable ski experiences in Utah. Its gourmet dining, beautiful lodges, and top-tier amenities make it ideal for families, beginners, or anyone wanting a premium trip. Choose Deer Valley if you value comfort, quality, and perfectly groomed skiing.

Solitude lives up to its name with quiet slopes, short lift lines, and a relaxed vibe that feels miles away from the busy Wasatch. It offers a great balance of terrain for all levels, including hidden powder stashes, scenic tree runs, and a cozy village atmosphere. Solitude is especially loved for its uncrowded feel and reliable snow, making it perfect for those who want to escape the hustle. Choose Solitude if you want peaceful skiing and authentic mountain charm.
